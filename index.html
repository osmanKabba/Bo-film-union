Welcome to Bo Film Union

About Us

The headquarters of BO Film Union shall be located at

Dr. Margai Street Durba Ground Section, Bo City,
Southern Province, Sierra Leone.

The jurisdiction of the BO Film Union shall extend to Sierra Leone, South, Bo District, Bo City.
The Bo Film Union is dedicated to bringing together filmmakers in the Southern Province. We cover four districts:
Bo, Pujehun, Monyaba, and Bonth. Our goal is to promote the growth of the film industry in Sierra Leone.

Our Mission

The mission of BO Film Union is to empower and protect our members by promoting fair and just workplaces,
advocating for workers' rights, and fostering solidarity among all workers. We are dedicated to improving
the quality of life for our members and their families while striving for social and economic justice.

Our Vision

We envision a thriving film industry in Sierra Leone, where filmmakers are empowered to tell their stories
and share their culture with the world.
